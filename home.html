Welcome to the
Computer Science Department

  • Short Courses

    Computer science department offer short courses that focus on specific topics within the field. These courses provide an in-depth exploration of specialized subjects such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity,
    computer graphics, software engineering, web development, database management,
    or computer networks.

  • Undergradute Courses

    This course serves as an introduction to the fundamental concepts of computer science. It covers topics such as programming fundamentals,
    algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving techniques. Students typically gain hands-on experience with
    programming languages like Python, Java, or C++.Undergraduate courses offered by the computer science
    department provide students with a solid foundation in key concepts, theories, and practical skills in the field of computer science.

  • MSc & MPhil Programmes

    MSc (Master of Science) and MPhil (Master of Philosophy) programs in computer science provide students with advanced knowledge, research skills, and specialized expertise in the field. These programs prepare graduates for careers in research, academia, industry, or further study at the doctoral level
    The programs offer a balance between theoretical foundations, practical skills, and research
    experience to equip students with the necessary tools to contribute to the ever-evolving field of computer science.

  • PhD Programms

    PhD (Doctor of Philosophy) programs in computer science are designed to produceindependent researchers and scholars who can make significant contributions to the field. Through rigorous research, coursework, and scholarly engagement, PhD students develop advanced expertise, critical thinking skills, and the ability to conduct innovative research that advances the boundaries of computer science knowledge.
